Visit Us

Join us for the Techcini Light Show in Charleston, SC! Enjoy a festive holiday light display that runs every evening during December.

  • Location: Great Egret Drive, Johns Island, SC
  • Operating Hours: Open daily from 5:00 PM to 10:00 PM throughout December.
  • Showtimes: Shows run every 30 minutes starting at 5:00 PM, and continue until 10:00 PM.
